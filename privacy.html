Privacy Policy

How NexStep handles shopper data.

This site stores limited local shopping data on the device you use and may collect form submissions you choose to send.

Local Storage

NexStep uses local browser storage to remember cart items, saved wishlist products, cookie preferences, and locally confirmed order history.

Contact Forms

When you submit a support or feedback form, the entered information is stored locally for this demo storefront experience.

Cookies

Cookie preferences are stored locally when you accept or decline the banner. See the cookies consent page for more detail.

User Choice

You may clear locally stored site data through your browser settings if you no longer want the device to remember bag, wishlist, or order activity.
