How NexStep remembers your shopping preferences.

This storefront uses local browser storage to make the experience more convenient across pages.

What is stored

Cookie consent status, cart contents, wishlist saves, contact-form submissions, and locally confirmed orders may be stored on your device.

Why it is used

These preferences help preserve the multi-page shopping experience without requiring account sign-in or a server-side backend.

Your control

You can clear local storage through browser settings if you want to reset the site state on your device.
