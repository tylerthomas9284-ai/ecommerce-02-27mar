Return Policy

Clear guidance for returns and exchanges.

This policy is written for U.S. shoppers who want to understand eligibility, timing, and the proper support path before submitting a request.

Return Window

Most unworn footwear may be reviewed for return or exchange within 30 days of delivery, subject to inspection and policy compliance.

Condition Requirements

Items should be returned in clean, unworn condition with original packaging where possible. Damaged, heavily worn, or altered items may be declined.

How to Start

Contact support@nexstep.site or use the contact form and include your order number and reason for the request.

Refund Timing

Approved refunds are typically processed back to the original payment method after inspection. Timing varies by payment provider.

Important Notes
Shipping fees may be non-refundable unless required by law or caused by a NexStep fulfillment issue.Exchange approval depends on stock availability at the time the request is reviewed.Questions about specific orders should be directed to the support team before sending items back.
